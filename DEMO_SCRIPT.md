# Hackade Demo Script

## Overview
The demo walks through the full hacker journey on Hackade: from profile setup through team matching and deployment. Two paths are available from the quest map.

---

## Scene 0: Profile Setup
**What it shows:** First-time profile creation with LinkedIn quick-import.

1. The demo starts on the Profile Setup screen
2. Click **"Import from LinkedIn"** - fields auto-fill one by one with animation (name, title, skills, bio)
3. Point out that skills can be manually edited (add/remove tags)
4. Click **"Continue"** to advance

**Talking points:**
- "We want to minimize friction - one click and your profile is ready"
- "Skills drive the matching algorithm throughout the platform"

---

## Scene 1a: Quest Map
**What it shows:** Choose a sponsor quest track or go mercenary.

### Quest Path
1. Three quest cards are visible (Google Finance AI, Sustainability Dashboard, Accessibility Toolkit)
2. Click the **Google Finance AI Agents Quest** card (first, glowing one)
3. This advances to the swipe/match flow

### Mercenary Path (alternate)
1. Instead of picking a quest, click **"Mercenary Mode"** at the bottom
2. This shows project cards from ALL quests, ranked by your profile skills
3. Same swipe mechanic, but broader pool of projects

**Talking points:**
- "Quests are sponsor tracks - structured paths with prizes"
- "Mercenary Mode is for hackers who want to skip the quest and just find the best skill-match across everything"

---

## Scene 1b: Swipe & Match
**What it shows:** Tinder-style project matching.

1. Swipe left (pass) or right (interested) on project cards
2. Drag cards or use the thumbs up/down buttons
3. On the second right-swipe, a match is triggered

---

## Scene 1c: Match Reveal
**What it shows:** Celebratory match animation.

1. "IT'S A MATCH!" screen with particle effects
2. Shows you matched with the AI-Powered Tamagotchi project
3. Click **"Join the Team"** to continue

---

## Scene 2: Character Select
**What it shows:** Team role claiming (RPG-style).

1. Team members are shown with their roles and skills
2. Claim your role on the team

---

## Scene 3: War Room
**What it shows:** Provisioned workspace with all tools ready.

1. Resources auto-provision one by one (Chat space, Figma, GitHub, API keys)
2. Vibe coding terminal shows AI-assisted code generation

---

## Scene 4: Safety Net
**What it shows:** Pre-submission checklist and guardrails.

1. Review submission requirements
2. Ensure all criteria are met before deploying

---

## Scene 5: Deploy Flow
**What it shows:** Final submission and deployment.

1. Submit your project
2. Celebratory completion screen

---

## Navigation Tips
- **Arrow Right** key advances through scenes
- **Scene dots** (bottom-right) allow jumping to any scene
- Dot 1 = Profile, Dots 2-6 = Scenes 1-5
- Both quest path and mercenary path converge at the Match Reveal and continue identically from there
